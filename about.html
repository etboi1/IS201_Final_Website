For All Ye Newbie Nerds, What Even Is The Cosmere?

First off, I'm sorry, but you ought to be ashamed that you don't know what The Cosmere is! It is the single most amazing thing of our time.

If you've read any books by Isaac Asimov (author of I, Robot) then you might be familiar with how many of his books, whether they were Robot novels or Foundation novels, were eventually revealed to be in the same universe. It was from Asimov that Brandon Sanderson drew inspiration to create several series of epic fantasy novels all set in the same universe, that will ultimately come to an incredible cross-over ending.

That is The Cosmere.

While not all of Brandon Sanderson's books are set in The Cosmere, the vast majority are, including, but not limited to, The Stormlight Archives, Mistborn, Elantris, and Warbreaker. All books and series featured on this website are, of course, set in The Cosmere.
